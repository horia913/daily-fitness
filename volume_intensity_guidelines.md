## Table 4: Aerobic Endurance (Heart Rate Zone Training)

| Category | Difficulty | Zone 2 Sessions/Week | Zone 4-5 Sessions/Week | Zone 2 Duration (min) Min | Zone 2 Duration (min) Max | Zone 4-5 Intervals (min) | HR % Max Zone 2 (Min) | HR % Max Zone 2 (Max) | HR % Max Zone 4-5 (Min) | HR % Max Zone 4-5 (Max) |
|----------|-----------|-------------------|---------------------|----------------------|----------------------|---------------------|-------------------|-------------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Aerobic Endurance | Beginner | 3 | 1 | 20 | 40 | 3-5 | 60 | 70 | 85 | 90 | 
| Aerobic Endurance | Intermediate | 4 | 2 | 30 | 60 | 4-8 | 60 | 75 | 85 | 92 |
| Aerobic Endurance | Advanced | 4-5 | 2-3 | 45 | 90 | 5-10 | 65 | 75 | 85 | 95 |
| Aerobic Endurance | Athlete | 5-6 | 2-3 | 60 | 120 | 6-12 | 65 | 75 | 88 | 100 |

**Heart Rate Zones:**
- Zone 1 (Recovery): 50-60% HR max
- Zone 2 (Aerobic Base): 60-75% HR max - **Should be 75-80% of total training time**
- Zone 3 (Tempo): 75-85% HR max - **Minimize time here (grey zone)**
- Zone 4 (Threshold): 85-92% HR max
- Zone 5 (VO2 Max): 92-100% HR max

**Polarized Training Distribution:**
- 75-80% of time in Zone 2 (easy)
- <10% in Zone 3 (minimize)
- 15-20% in Zones 4-5 (hard)
